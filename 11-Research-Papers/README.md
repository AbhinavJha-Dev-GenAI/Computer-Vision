1. **The Abstract**: What is the problem and the high-level solution?
2. **The Introduction**: Why is this problem important?
3. **The Methodology**: Focus on the architecture diagrams.
4. **The Results**: How does it compare to previous SOTA?
5. **Conclusion**: What are the limitations?
